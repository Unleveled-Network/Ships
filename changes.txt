
v0.9 beta change log

Added a new Berth block that looks like a bed, but it works on ships!
Sail detection was redesigned to make side-facing sails more useful
Grass now correctly saves its biome color when you launch/dock ships
Ship clipboard now copies hanging entities (item frames, paintings, ship plaques, etc)
Ferns, vines, tall grass, etc now correctly launch with ships
Fixed a bug in the renderer that would not render more than one launched ship at a time
Fixed a bug that would spawn items from invisible blocks
Fixed multiple bugs with water displacement inside ship hulls
Non-solid blocks under the water line now correctly remain part of ships during launching/docking
Various crash fixes
Block limit is now 10,000 blocks!
